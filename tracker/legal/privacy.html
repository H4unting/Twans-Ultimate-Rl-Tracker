This document describes how Twans Ultimate Tracker (“the Service”, “we”, “us”) handles information. It is provided for transparency and is not legal advice. We do not claim any specific regulatory certification (such as GDPR, CCPA, or SOC 2 compliance).

1. Who we are

Twans Ultimate Tracker is an independent personal improvement tracker for Rocket League and Valorant match logging and statistics. The operator is Anthony, independent developer of Twans Ultimate Tracker.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the Service, we may process:

  • Account data — email address, authentication identifiers, and profile fields you provide (display name, bio, colors, optional avatar).
  • Game & match data — logged match results, ranks/MMR or RR, agents, maps, tags, notes, session numbers, and related statistics you enter or that are sent via optional local auto-log tools.
  • Settings & preferences — goals, active game, rank baselines, UI preferences stored in your account settings.
  • Squad data — squad names, invite codes, membership roles, and shared stats visible to squad members per app permissions.
  • Technical data — standard web logs from our hosting and database providers (e.g. IP address, user agent, request timestamps) as part of normal infrastructure operation.
  • Local browser data — session tokens and preferences may be stored in your browser (localStorage / sessionStorage) for sign-in and convenience.

We do not intentionally collect payment card data — the Service is not a paid storefront at this time.

3. How we use information

  • Provide and maintain your account and synced data across devices.
  • Display statistics, dashboards, squads, and coaching insights you request.
  • Authenticate you and protect accounts (via Supabase Auth).
  • Improve reliability, security, and bugs (based on errors you or we observe).

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Authentication providers

The Service uses Supabase Auth for account management. Sign-in options may include:

  • Email and password — credentials are handled by Supabase; we do not store plain-text passwords in app code.
  • Google OAuth — if you choose Google sign-in, Google processes your authentication according to Google’s policies. We receive basic profile information (such as email and name/avatar URL) needed to create your account.

Third-party auth providers have their own privacy policies independent of this Service.

5. Storage & processing (Supabase)

Account and match data are stored in a Supabase project (PostgreSQL database and optional Storage buckets). Data is associated with your user ID and protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies intended to limit access to your own records and squad-shared views.

Profile avatars may be stored in a Supabase Storage bucket (avatars) or, if storage is unavailable, as a compressed image reference in your profile record. Squad members with permission may see your display name and avatar in squad views.

Supabase acts as our infrastructure provider. Their handling of data is subject to Supabase’s privacy policy. Database region and subprocessors depend on your Supabase project configuration.

6. Local auto-log (optional)

Optional desktop bridge / auto-log tools run on your computer and may read game-related data locally before sending match summaries to the Service. That local processing is not stored on our servers except for the match records you choose to save.

7. Cookies & similar technologies

The Service uses browser storage for authentication sessions and preferences. We do not operate third-party advertising cookies. Third-party CDNs (e.g. Chart.js) may set their own caching behavior when loading scripts.

8. Data retention

We retain your data while your account is active and as needed to operate the Service. You may delete individual match logs in the app. Account deletion is described below.

9. Your choices & rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete personal data. We will try to honor reasonable requests, subject to technical and legal limits.

  • Access / export — use in-app export features where available, or email us.
  • Correction — edit profile and match data in the app.
  • Deletion — see Section 10.
  • Withdraw consent — stop using the Service and request account deletion.

We may need to verify your identity before processing requests.

10. Data deletion requests

In the app: Profile → type DELETE → Delete my account to permanently remove your cloud data and sign-in access (requires the operator to enable server-side deletion).

By email: contact anthonyinf354332@gmail.com from the address linked to your account with the subject line Account Deletion Request.

We will aim to delete or anonymize account data within a reasonable period after verification. Backups, logs, and legal retention requirements may delay complete removal.

Deleting your account does not automatically delete copies squad mates may have seen during membership; squad admin features may retain historical references until squads are updated.
